What We'll Offer To Everyone.....

Free

Room Service

The room service will be free of cost. Proper care of the homeless person will be taken under our observation. The rooms are cleaned and sanitized daily so that they feel safe to stay there. Moreover, a team of doctorr and nurses are also kept in case any mishappening happens.

Free

Food & Drinks

The food that we'll provide to homeless people will be also free of cost. Food here will be cooked by considering proper hygiene and health of that person. A nutritionist will be appointed so that food is cooked according to the need of the person. Moreover, If a person eats healthy, he stays healthy. This our motto and to help every homeless person is our mission.
